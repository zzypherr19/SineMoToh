WEDNESDAY (2022–)

Directed by Alfred Gough and Miles Millar

A supernatural comedy-horror coming-of-age American television series that is based on Charles Addams' Wednesday Addams Wednesday Addams attends Nevermore Academy while trying to control her developing psychic gift, stop a killing spree, and solve the mystery that ensnared her parents 25 years ago. 


Starring

Jenna Ortega


Emma Myers


Hunter Doohan


Review

I can't imagine a more perfect casting for Wednesday. The plot is a little hard to follow, and people expecting this to be an Addams Family reboot will not be happy, but the show stands on its own. It's enjoyable and unique, and definitely worth watching if only for Ortega's performance. The cinematography is beautiful as well; everything is catered around Wednesday, so she stands out in every scene, even in a school full of "outcasts." I especially enjoyed the juxtaposition between Wednesday and her bubbly roommate. People who enjoyed Charlie and the Chocolate Factory and A Series of Unfortunate Events will love this show.
