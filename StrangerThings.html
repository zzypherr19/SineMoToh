Review

Stranger Things is absolutely as good as everyone says it is. When a show is as talked about as this one has been, it's hard to live up to expectations. Stranger Things has not only met those expectations but has surpassed them in every way. This is the show that puts Netflix on the map as far as original shows go, as this was not only one of their first but also their biggest. As much as I love this show, it's probably time for it to end, and it is. This upcoming season is going to be the last. We have literally watched this cast grow up; they were children when this show first started, and now they're all in their 20s. I can't wait for this last season and am going to miss it when it's gone.
